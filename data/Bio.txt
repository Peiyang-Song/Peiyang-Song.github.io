Peiyang Song is an undergraduate student studying Computer Science at California Institute of Technology (Caltech). He is currently a researcher in the Stanford AI Lab (SAIL), advised by Prof. Noah Goodman in the Computation & Cognition Lab (CoCoLab). During the past, he has also worked with Prof. Anima Anandkumar (Caltech), Dr. Kaiyu Yang (Meta), Prof. Tim Sherwood (UC Santa Barbara), and Dr. Jeremy Lau (Google). His research interest is mainly in machine reasoning, especially AI for mathematics and code generation. In the past, he also worked on energy-efficient machine learning systems.
